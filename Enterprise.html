High Operational Costs

High operational costs can limit business growth. Dukaansarthi offers cost-effective solutions, helping retailers reduce expenses and focus on core business activities.

How does Dukaansarthi ERP handle GST compliance?

+

Dukaansarthi ERP is fully GST-compliant and helps businesses manage their tax obligations efficiently. The system automatically calculates GST rates, generates GST-compliant invoices, and maintains accurate records for GST returns. Our built-in GST features ensure your business stays compliant with all regulations.

+

Yes, Dukaansarthi ERP offers cloud-based access, allowing you to manage your business from anywhere with an internet connection. Multiple users can access the system simultaneously, making it perfect for businesses with multiple locations or remote teams.
